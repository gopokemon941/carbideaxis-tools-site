Supply Range & Model-List Quotation

Use this page to check which product families we cover and how to request a quote when your model list contains many items.

Supply range and model-list quotation support

Our product range covers common carbide insert and cutting tool families. If you have a long model list, send the codes directly and we will check standard replacements and available supply.

Turning inserts

Common families include CNMG, WNMG, TNMG, DNMG, SNMG, VNMG, CCMT, DCMT, VBMT and other standard turning inserts for steel, stainless steel, cast iron and non-ferrous machining.

Milling inserts

Common families include APMT, APKT, SEKT, RPMT, RDMT, RDKW, XOMX, ONHU and other popular milling insert series used in face milling, shoulder milling and copy milling.

Threading & grooving inserts

Threading inserts, laydown thread inserts, full-profile / partial-profile types, grooving inserts, parting inserts and related holders can all be checked by code or drawing.

PCD / CBN / cermet

PCD inserts for non-ferrous and abrasive materials, CBN inserts for hardened steel and cast iron, and cermet inserts for stable finishing are available in common shapes and special-use directions.

Carbide end mills

Solid carbide end mills can usually be checked by diameter, flute count, overall length, flute length, neck / shank type and coating. MOQ for end mills can start from 1 pc.

OEM supply

If you buy by drawing, sample or mixed model list, we can sort the quotation by product family first, then confirm MOQ, packing and lead time model by model.
