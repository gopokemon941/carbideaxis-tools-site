Application Examples by Material and Operation

These are the application directions we quote most often: cast iron, superalloy, hardened steel, non-ferrous machining, glass / wood / composites, automotive wheel hubs and other special jobs.

Applications we quote most often

Send the tool code, workpiece material, operation, machine condition and current problem. That is usually enough to start the first quotation and tool suggestion.

Cast iron machining

Common requests include turning and milling inserts for gray iron and nodular iron with focus on wear life, edge stability and dry cutting performance.

High-temperature alloy / superalloy

For Inconel and similar alloys, buyers usually send speed, feed, depth of cut, coolant condition and the current failure mode before grade selection.

Hardened steel

CBN inserts and other hard-turning solutions are usually quoted by hardness range, interrupted cutting condition and surface finish target.

Non-ferrous materials

PCD inserts and sharp-edged tooling are commonly requested for aluminum, copper and similar materials where finish and burr control matter.

Glass, wood and composite materials

PCD or special wear-resistant tooling can be checked for fiber-rich, abrasive or brittle materials. Send the workpiece and edge quality requirement.

Automotive wheel hubs

Typical RFQs include aluminum wheel hub turning and milling where finish, consistency and burr control are key points.

Other special requests

Mixed model lists, material-based requests, neutral packaging, private label packaging, toolholder matching and OEM supply projects can all be discussed through our partner factory network.
