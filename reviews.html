How we help every buyer get a better sourcing experience

Every RFQ is different. To reduce mismatch and save time, we check the product code, application details, MOQ, packing and follow-up points before the order is confirmed.

1

Understand the request first

Send the ISO code, drawing, sample photo, model list or current cutting problem. We first confirm what needs to be matched.

2

3
